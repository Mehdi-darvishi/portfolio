About Me

My Story.

Greetings! I am Mehdi Darvishi, a dedicated student at Shiraz University of Technology (SUTech) with a passion for Antennas & Radar, Microwave components, Microwave & ultrasound imaging, and Medical equipment. As far as I can remember, my mind has always been fascinated with satellites, radio/TV broadcasting, and the way we communicate with each other. In fact, my oldest brother was the primary influence, who has worked as a telecommunication technician on antennas, particularly the reflectors. On occasion, he took me to his workplace, where I had the thrilling opportunity to interact with several components such as LNBs, Waveguides, PCBs, and more. . These experiences, coupled with my strong aptitude for mathematics, made me determined to pursue Electrical Engineering as my major. Consequently, I tried my best to be admitted to the best universities in Iran, and I chose Shiraz University of Technology (SUTech), renowned as one of the most competitive universities for Electrical Engineering studies in the country. The first years of my bachelor’s study were not quite as exciting as I expected. Although taking theoretical and fundamental courses did not satisfy my executive personality, I remained motivated. It was during the second and third years of my undergraduate study that I finally got the chance to take courses pertinent to electric circuits, electromagnetic waves, and communications, such as “Field & Waves, Microwave I, Communications I, and Analysis of Signals and Systems”. I found these courses truly fascinating among other courses that were offered. Finally, I realized that Electrical Engineering is the field that exactly matches my academic and personal interests.
